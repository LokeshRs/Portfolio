TagMe!

I conceived and brought to life 'TagMe' during my college journey, a dynamic web platform designed to foster unity among sports enthusiasts. As the visionary behind TagMe, I envisioned a space where players could effortlessly come together to enjoy their favorite sports. The platform enables users to search for and join existing games while also empowering them to create their own sporting events, complete with customizable details such as sport type, location, and time. 'TagMe' facilitates networking and community building by connecting like-minded individuals and providing real-time updates on game availability. This project reflects my dedication to creating practical solutions that bring people together through shared interests and experiences.
